Peter Mahony Art

"I am a landscape artist, I work in gouache, acrylics and oils and I've painted for a number of years now, developing my craft and finding form for my work.For some time I was interested in the American style Tonalism wherein I would paint landscapes from imagination that were focused on limited colours and subject matter.

Now I have moved on from that and have become more engaged with working from nature with an emphasis on colour theory. While Tonalism was limiting, working from nature provides much more room to grow. I want my newer works to feel indicative of the Irish landscape, capturing its beauty and its bleakness."
